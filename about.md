::: page-content
Welcome to Xania Books, the home of great reading.

 

Established in 2019, Xania Books publishes an eclectic range of fiction
and non-fiction books ranging from Fitness and Weight Loss, Time
Management, Slug Prevention ... or would you prefer to enter the dark
world of vampires in 'The Selenics' series?

 

Perhaps uniquely, all books published here at Xania Books can be bought
at different prices in paperback, kindle and PDF formats. Other e-reader
formats are also available.
